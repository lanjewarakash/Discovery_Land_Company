Iron Horse
Golf Club

Scroll to explore

Whitefish, Montana

Escape to the mountains at Iron Horse Golf Club, located in beautiful Whitefish, Montana. This year-round residential community is minutes away from the Glacier National Park.

A mountain retreat
for everyone

Play golf against a dramatic backdrop of the rugged Montana Rockies on our Tom Fazio-designed course or enjoy our Outdoor Pursuits program that caters to members of all ages. Experience horseback riding in Glacier National Park or if you're more of a thrill-seeker test your courage white-water rafting on the Flathead River. The Iron Horse experience is one of relaxation and enjoyment for all the family.
